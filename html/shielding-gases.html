Welding Gas

Shielding Gases (Argon & Mixes)

High-quality shielding gases for MIG and TIG welding applications.

Product Overview

Shielding gases are critical for arc welding processes to protect the weld pool from atmospheric contamination. We supply pure Argon, Carbon Dioxide (CO2), and Argon/CO2 mixtures tailored to your specific material and process requirements.

Available Types

  • Pure Argon (99.99%)
  • Industrial CO2
  • Argon + 20% CO2
  • Argon + 5% O2
  • Helium Mixtures
  • Cylinder Sizes: 10L, 47L, 50L

Selection Guide

  • Pure Argon: Best for TIG welding (all metals) and MIG welding of aluminum.
  • Argon/CO2 Mix: The standard for MIG welding mild steel (MAG). Reduces spatter and improves penetration.
  • Pure CO2: Low-cost option for MIG welding mild steel. Higher spatter but deep penetration.

Unsure which gas to use?

Our welding experts can recommend the perfect gas mixture to optimize your weld quality and productivity.
