Gas Types & Applications

Oxygen O₂

Supports combustion and essential for life support and industrial processes.

Industries

  • Healthcare and hospitals
  • Metal fabrication and welding
  • Water treatment facilities
  • Chemical manufacturing
  • Aerospace

Why It's Important

Oxygen is critical for sustaining life in medical settings and enables high-temperature processes in industrial applications. In welding, it increases flame temperature for cutting and joining metals.

RM40.00(Refill ≤ 10L)

Nitrogen N₂

Inert gas used for purging, blanketing, and creating oxygen-free environments.

Industries

  • Food and beverage
  • Electronics manufacturing
  • Pharmaceutical production
  • Oil and gas
  • Chemical processing

Why It's Important

Nitrogen prevents spoilage and contamination by displacing oxygen. In food packaging, it extends shelf life. In electronics, it prevents oxidation during manufacturing.

RM40.00(Refill ≤ 10L)

Argon Ar

Noble gas primarily used as a shielding gas in welding and inert atmospheres.

Industries

  • Metal fabrication (TIG/MIG)
  • Specialty metals (titanium)
  • Electronics production
  • Light bulb manufacturing
  • Scientific research

Why It's Important

Argon's complete inertness protects welds from atmospheric contamination, resulting in stronger, cleaner welds. For specialty metals like titanium, argon shielding is absolutely necessary.

RM40.00(Refill ≤ 10L)

Carbon Dioxide CO₂

Used for carbonation, refrigeration, pH control, and welding shielding.

Industries

  • Food and beverage
  • Welding (MIG shielding)
  • Water treatment
  • Fire suppression systems
  • Greenhouses

Why It's Important

CO₂ gives beverages their fizz and is essential for food preservation as dry ice. In welding, it's a cost-effective shielding gas. Its ability to regulate pH makes it valuable in water treatment.

RM40.40(Refill ≤ 10L)

Helium He

* SERVICE ONLY AVAILABLE AT HQ (PASIR GUDANG)

Used for leak detection, cooling, controlled atmospheres, and analytics.

Industries

  • Healthcare (MRI machines)
  • Aerospace and aviation
  • Diving (mixed gases)
  • Manufacturing leak detection
  • Scientific research

Why It's Important

Helium is the only substance cold enough to cool superconducting magnets in MRI machines. Its small atomic size makes it perfect for detecting tiny leaks.

RM40.00(Refill ≤ 10L)

Medical Oxygen Med

Pharmaceutical-grade oxygen for patient therapy and life support.

Industries

  • Hospitals and clinics
  • Emergency services
  • Home healthcare
  • Dental offices
  • Veterinary medicine

Why It's Important

Medical oxygen meets strict purity standards (typically 99.5%+) regulated by health authorities. It's essential for treating respiratory conditions, surgery support, and emergency resuscitation.
