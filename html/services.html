Gas Types & Applications

Breathing Air (BA)

Clean, filtered compressed air for breathing systems and industrial use.

Industries

Healthcare, Metal Fabrication, Water Treatment, Chemical Manufacturing, Aerospace.

Why It's Important

Provides a safe air supply for SCBA and airline systems in hazardous environments.

BA

Nitrogen (N₂)

Inert gas used for purging, blanketing, and creating oxygen-free environments.

Industries

Food & Beverage, Electronics, Pharmaceuticals, Oil & Gas, Chemical Processing.

Why It's Important

Prevents spoilage and contamination by displacing oxygen, extending shelf life.

N₂

Argon (Ar)

Noble gas primarily used as a shielding gas in welding and inert atmospheres.

Industries

Metal Fabrication (TIG/MIG), Electronics, Research.

Why It's Important

Protects welds from atmospheric contamination, resulting in stronger, cleaner welds.

Ar
