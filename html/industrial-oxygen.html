Breathing Air

Clean, filtered compressed air for SCBA filling, airline systems, and industrial applications.

Product Overview

Our Breathing Air is conditioned and filtered to meet commonly accepted breathing air quality requirements for use with SCBA/EEBD and airline respirator systems. It also serves general industrial needs where clean, dry compressed air is required.

Technical Specifications

  • Quality: Clean, dry compressed air
  • Supply: Cylinders or bulk systems
  • Cylinder Sizes: 10L, 47L, 50L
  • Working Pressure: 150–200 bar
  • Typical Use: SCBA/airline systems

Key Applications

  • Respiratory Safety: Filling SCBA/EEBD cylinders; airline respirator systems.
  • Maintenance & Confined Space: Safe air supply for plant operations.
  • Industrial Air: Clean, dry air for pneumatic tools and process use.
