Chem-Pro Nitrile Gloves

22mil 18" unlined nitrile gloves providing excellent chemical resistance.

Product Overview

Chem-Pro nitrile gloves deliver long-cuff forearm protection and strong resistance against a wide range of chemicals, making them suitable for handling solvents, oils, and hazardous liquids in industrial environments.

Typical Specifications

  • Material: Nitrile, unlined
  • Thickness: 22 mil
  • Length: 18 inch (approx. 45 cm)
  • Textured grip for wet handling
  • Excellent oil and solvent resistance
  • Available in multiple sizes

Typical Applications

  • Chemical processing: Handling acids, caustics, and solvents.
  • Maintenance: Equipment cleaning and degreasing tasks.
  • Laboratory work: Sample preparation and handling of hazardous liquids.
