MSA ALTAIR 4XR Multigas Detector

The most rugged 4-gas detector on the market, equipped with XCell sensors.

Product Overview

The ALTAIR 4XR is an extremely durable multigas detector that can withstand drops up to 7.5 meters and is IP68 rated. Equipped with rapid-response MSA XCell® sensors, it provides real-time alerts through Bluetooth connectivity to the MSA ALTAIR Connect app. It is designed for those who work in the most demanding environments.

Technical Specifications

  • Gases: LEL, O2, CO, H2S, NO2, SO2
  • Drop Test: 7.5m (25ft) concrete
  • Temperature: -40°F to +140°F
  • Run Time: 24 hours @ 25°C
  • Ingress Protection: IP68 (2m, 1hr)
  • Warranty: 4-year instrument & sensor

Key Features

  • XCell Sensor Technology: Sensors respond 2x faster than the industry average, allowing for quicker evacuation and increased safety.
  • MotionAlert™: Man-down alarm triggers if the user becomes immobile, notifying others of a potential emergency.
  • InstantAlert™: Allows users to manually signal others about a dangerous situation with a single button press.
  • Rugged Durability: Exceeds MIL-STD-810G drop test requirements, making it the toughest detector in its class.
