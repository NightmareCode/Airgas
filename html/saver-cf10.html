Product Overview

The Dräger Saver CF10 provides a reliable 10-minute supply of breathing air to enable safe escape from toxic or oxygen-deficient environments. Its compact design and automatic activation make it an essential safety component for refineries, chemical plants, and confined space operations.

Technical Specifications

  • Duration: 10 minutes
  • Weight: ~4.2kg (Soft Bag)
  • Cylinder: 2.0L Aluminum/Steel
  • Flow Rate: 35 L/min
  • Standards: EN 1146, ISO 23269
  • Storage: Soft Bag or Hard Case

Key Features

  • Instant Activation: The system is automatically triggered upon opening the carrying bag, providing immediate respiratory protection.
  • Lightweight & Compact: Designed for portability, allowing users to carry it easily during their daily work in high-risk areas.
  • Wide Vision Visor: The high-visibility hood features a large visor for an unobstructed field of view during emergency evacuation.
  • Durable Construction: Built to withstand harsh industrial environments, with optional anti-static bags for explosive zones (ATEX).
