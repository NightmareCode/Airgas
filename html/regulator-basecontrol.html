GCE BaseControl Regulator

Precision gas pressure regulator for safe and accurate gas delivery from cylinders.

Product Overview

The GCE BaseControl® is a robust and precise cylinder regulator conforming to ISO 2503 standards. Designed for safe operation with compressed gases up to 230 bar filling pressure, it is suitable for a wide range of industrial, laboratory, and welding applications where accurate pressure control is critical.

Technical Specifications

  • Inlet Pressure: Up to 230 bar
  • Outlet Pressure: 0 – 10 bar
  • Body Material: Forged Brass
  • Standards: ISO 2503, ISO 5171
  • Gases: O2, Ar, CO2, C2H2, C3H8
  • Inlet Filter: Sintered Brass

Key Features

  • Precision Control: High-quality internal components ensure consistent delivery pressure regardless of cylinder volume.
  • Enhanced Safety: Features an integrated pressure relief valve (PRV) to protect against low-pressure chamber over-pressurization.
  • Easy Readability: Dual-scale gauges (bar/psi) with a 50mm diameter provide clear monitoring of both cylinder and delivery pressure.
  • Robust Construction: Forged brass body and high-pressure sintered filter for long-term reliability in industrial environments.
