BaseControl Regulator

Precision single-stage cylinder regulator for safe and accurate control of industrial gases.

⚙️

Product Overview

The GCE BaseControl is a professional-grade single-stage regulator designed for high-pressure gas cylinders. It provides stable outlet pressure and flow, ensuring safety and precision in various industrial and laboratory applications.

Technical Specifications

  • Inlet Pressure: Up to 300 bar
  • Outlet Pressure: 0 - 10 bar
  • Flow Rate: up to 30 m³/h
  • Body Material: Forged Brass
  • Bonnet Material: Zn-Al alloy
  • Approval: ISO 2503

Key Features

  • Robust Design: Forged brass body provides maximum durability and safety under high pressure.
  • Precise Control: High-quality diaphragm ensures stable delivery pressure throughout the cylinder life.
  • Clear Readout: Equipped with large, easy-to-read pressure gauges for both cylinder and outlet pressure.
  • Safety Valve: Integrated safety relief valve prevents over-pressurization of downstream equipment.
