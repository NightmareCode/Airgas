About Airgas Technology

Your trusted partner in industrial gases, safety, and firefighting solutions.

Since 1996

Our Story

AIRGAS TECHNOLOGY SDN BHD (199601033308 / 405660-D) is a premier Malaysian supplier with a rich history of providing high-quality industrial gases, safety equipment, and firefighting solutions. Our journey began with a commitment to excellence, and today, we are proud to be a trusted partner for industries across the nation.

Our Mission

"To service local industries as well as act as a stockist for an extensive range of renowned products, providing superior quality and purity at reasonable and competitive prices."

What We Do

Industrial Gases

We supply a wide range of industrial gases, including oxygen, nitrogen, and argon, ensuring purity and reliability for all your needs.

Safety Equipment

Our extensive range of safety equipment includes PPE, SCBA, and gas detection systems to ensure a safe working environment.

Firefighting Solutions

We provide comprehensive firefighting solutions, including extinguishers, CO2 systems, and a range of fire-suppressing media.
