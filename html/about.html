About Airgas Technology

Local Malaysian partner for industrial gases, safety, and firefighting solutions.

Since 1996

Company Overview

AIRGAS TECHNOLOGY SDN BHD (199601033308 / 405660-D) is a premier Malaysian supplier specializing in industrial gases, safety equipment, and firefighting solutions.

  • Industry Leaders Specializing in industrial gases, medical gases, and safety equipment.
  • Strategic Locations HQ in Johor Bahru and Branch in Nilai, Negeri Sembilan.
  • Certified Quality Products meet ANZI, CE, or EN standards for superior purity.

Our Mission

"To service local industries as well as act as a stockist for an extensive range of renowned products, providing superior quality and purity at reasonable and competitive prices."

Key Business Areas

Gases

Wide range of industrial gases including oxygen, acetylene, nitrogen, argon, CO2, and helium. Key supplier of medical gases like medical oxygen and nitrous oxide.

Gas Equipment

Cylinders, valves, regulators, hoses, and connectors. Authorized distributor for reputable manufacturers from Germany, Italy, China, and Korea.

Cut & Weld

Inverter welding machines, MIG/TIG welding, oxygen cutting equipment, electrodes, and grinding discs for all industrial applications.

Personal Protection

Complete PPE solutions including SCBA, EEBD, respirators, fireman suits, chemical suits, helmets, gloves, and safety shoes.

Firefighting

CO2 systems, extinguishers, trolleys, and media like FM200, ABC powder, and AFFF foam. Full service from refilling to inspection.

Equipment Rental

Cost-effective rental of SCBA, EEBD, gas detectors, and compressors for short-term projects to reduce upfront capital expenditure.

Workshop Services

Testing and inspection for fire detectors, gas detectors, cylinder pressure, valves, and respirators to ensure safety compliance.

Safety Training

Professional training programs for gas detectors, lifeline/BA systems, chemical suits, confined space entry, and firefighting.
