Our Story

Since 1996, AirGas Technology has been serving Malaysian industries as a trusted one-stop supplier of industrial gases, safety equipment, and firefighting solutions. From humble beginnings nearly 30 years ago, we operate two strategic locations in Johor Bahru and Nilai, serving industries across Malaysia.

Mission

To service local industries as a stockist for an extensive range of renowned products, providing superior quality and purity at reasonable and competitive prices.
