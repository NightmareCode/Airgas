LW 100E Breathing Air Compressor

Compact high-pressure breathing air compressor for SCBA and diving cylinder filling.

Product Overview

The LW 100E is a compact breathing air compressor designed for reliable filling of SCBA and diving cylinders. Its robust construction and integrated filtration system deliver breathing-quality air according to relevant standards.

Typical Specifications

  • Working pressure up to 225/330 bar
  • Free air delivery around 100 L/min
  • Single or three-phase electric motor
  • Integrated purification filter
  • Automatic condensate drain (optional)
  • Hour counter and safety controls

Typical Applications

  • Fire stations: Filling SCBA cylinders for firefighting teams.
  • Diving centres: Breathing air supply for scuba cylinders.
  • Industrial ERT: On-site breathing air for emergency response teams.
