CO2 Refilling & Transfer

Professional liquid CO2 transfer and cylinder refilling services.

Efficient CO2 Management

We provide specialized services for the transfer and refilling of Carbon Dioxide (CO2) for fire suppression systems, industrial cylinders, and beverage carbonation.

Handling liquid CO2 requires specialized high-pressure pumps and strict safety protocols to prevent freezing, over-pressurization, and cylinder failure. Our facility is equipped with industrial-grade CO2 transfer pumps to ensure safe and accurate filling.

Our Services Include:

  • Fire Extinguisher Refilling: Refilling 2kg, 5kg, and large trolley CO2 extinguishers.
  • Industrial Cylinder Filling: Filling standard 10L, 20L, and 45L CO2 cylinders for welding and industrial use.
  • System Maintenance: Servicing CO2 transfer pumps, checking seals, valves, and pressure gauges.
  • Leak Detection: Post-fill inspection to ensure valve integrity and seal tightness.
  • Purity Check: Ensuring the CO2 grade meets the required standard (Industrial vs Food Grade).

Why Professional Refilling?

CO2 is stored as a liquid under high pressure. Improper filling can lead to dangerous over-filling or insufficient pressure. Our weight-based filling process ensures every cylinder contains exactly the specified amount of product.
