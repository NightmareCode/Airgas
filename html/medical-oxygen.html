Medical Oxygen (O₂)

Certified medical grade oxygen for hospitals, clinics, and home care.

Product Overview

Our Medical Oxygen meets strict pharmaceutical standards and is critical for resuscitation, life support, and oxygen therapy. We provide timely refills and cylinder exchanges for healthcare facilities and home patients.

Specifications

  • Purity: ≥ 99.5% Medical Grade
  • Cylinder Color: White
  • Valve Type: Pin Index / Bullnose
  • Sizes: 1.4L, 2.8L, 4.6L, 10L, 47L
  • Pressure: 137 bar / 200 bar
  • License: MDA Registered

Key Uses

  • Respiratory Support: Treatment for COPD, pneumonia, and asthma.
  • Anesthesia: Carrier gas for anesthetic agents during surgery.
  • Emergency Care: Critical for trauma and resuscitation.
