Product Overview

Airgas Technology provides high-purity medical oxygen essential for healthcare facilities, emergency medical services, and home care. Our medical oxygen is produced and filled under strict quality controls to ensure it meets pharmaceutical and medical standards.

Technical Specifications

  • Purity: >99.5%
  • Grade: Medical Grade
  • Cylinder Sizes: 1.4L, 2.8L, 10L, 47L
  • Pressure: 150 bar / 200 bar
  • Valve: Pin Index / Bullnose
  • Color Code: White shoulder (Global standard)

Key Features

  • Quality Guaranteed: Every cylinder is batch-tested for purity and moisture content to ensure patient safety.
  • Reliable Supply: We offer efficient delivery and exchange services for hospitals, clinics, and individual users.
  • Safety First: Cylinders are regularly inspected and hydrostatically tested in compliance with local regulations.
  • Complete Solutions: We also provide medical regulators, masks, and flowmeters for a complete oxygen therapy setup.
