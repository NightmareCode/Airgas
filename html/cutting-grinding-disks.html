Cutting & Grinding Disks

High-quality abrasive disks for precise metal cutting and surface preparation.

Product Overview

Our selection of professional-grade cutting and grinding disks is engineered for performance, safety, and longevity. Designed to handle various metals including carbon steel, stainless steel, and alloys, these abrasives provide clean cuts and smooth finishes for industrial fabrication and maintenance tasks.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Aluminum Oxide / Zirconia
  • Sizes: 4", 5", 7", 9", 14"
  • Max RPM: Up to 15,300 RPM
  • Thickness: 1.0mm - 6.0mm
  • Application: Steel, Inox, Cast Iron
  • Safety: EN 12413 Compliant

Key Features

  • Long Service Life: Advanced grain bonding technology ensures minimal wear and maximum material removal per disk.
  • Precision Performance: Balanced construction reduces vibration, providing more control and cleaner results.
  • Safety First: Reinforced with high-tensile fiberglass mesh to prevent breakage during high-speed operations.
  • Versatile Range: Available in various thicknesses for thin cutting, heavy-duty grinding, and finishing.
