Gas Detector Calibration

Precision calibration services for portable and fixed gas detection systems.

Reliable Detection Saves Lives

We offer comprehensive calibration and maintenance services for gas detectors to ensure accurate readings of combustible gases (LEL), oxygen (O2), hydrogen sulfide (H2S), and carbon monoxide (CO).

Using advanced automated docking stations like the BW IntelliDoX and MSA Galaxy GX2, we perform precise bump tests and calibrations that meet manufacturer specifications. Regular calibration is essential to correct sensor drift and verify alarm functionality.

Our Calibration Services:

  • Bump Testing: A quick functional check to verify that sensors respond to gas and alarms activate.
  • Full Calibration: Adjustment of sensor response to match a known concentration of calibration gas.
  • Sensor Replacement: Replacement of expired or failed electrochemical and catalytic bead sensors.
  • Data Logging: Extraction of event logs and exposure history for safety audits.
  • Firmware Updates: Updating device software to the latest version for improved performance.
