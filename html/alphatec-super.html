Chemical Protection

AlphaTec SUPER Agents Protection

High-end chemical protective suit for work in aggressive chemical and biological environments.

Product Overview

AlphaTec SUPER suits are designed to protect emergency response teams and industrial workers from a wide range of aggressive chemicals and warfare agents. The fully encapsulating design offers excellent barrier properties while maintaining freedom of movement for critical tasks.

Typical Specifications

  • Type 1a gas-tight design
  • Highly chemical-resistant laminate
  • Integrated safety boots and gloves
  • Visor with wide field of view
  • SCBA compatible design
  • Sizes from S to XXL

Typical Applications

  • Hazmat response: Handling unknown chemical releases and spills.
  • Industrial cleaning: High-risk tank cleaning and maintenance work.
  • Chemical transfer: Loading and unloading hazardous liquids and gases.
