SCBA

Dräger PSS 7000

The ultimate professional SCBA for maximum comfort, stability, and durability in extreme conditions.

Product Overview

The Dräger PSS® 7000 is an outstanding breathing apparatus that provides maximum comfort and stability. Designed for the most rigorous demands, it features an ergonomic backplate and a unique self-adjusting pivoting waist belt that keeps the SCBA in position even when reaching or crawling.

Technical Specifications

  • Weight: 11.5 kg (with cylinder)
  • Backplate: Carbon fiber composite
  • Harness: PBI / Aramid blend
  • Adjustability: 3-position backplate
  • Standards: NFPA 1981/1982, EN 137
  • Monitoring: Sentinel 7000 Integrated

Key Features

  • 3-Position Backplate: Adjustable height to fit different torso lengths for personalized ergonomic comfort.
  • Pivoting Waist Belt: Automatically follows wearer movements, preventing the SCBA from shifting or riding up.
  • Lock-and-Load System: Rapid cylinder change-outs with a secure quarter-turn QuickConnect adapter.
  • Integrated Electronics: Centralized power supply and pressure sensor module for real-time monitoring.
