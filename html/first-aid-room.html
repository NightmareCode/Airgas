First Aid Room Setup

Complete medical station equipment for workplace first response and injury management.

Product Overview

A well-designed first aid room is essential for workplaces with high-risk operations or large employee populations. We provide complete setup solutions that comply with DOSH Malaysia guidelines, ensuring your facility is equipped to handle everything from minor injuries to critical emergency stabilizations before medical services arrive.

Standard Equipment Included

  • Medical Examination Couch
  • Portable Foldable Stretcher
  • Industrial First Aid Box
  • Medical Storage Cupboard
  • Sink with Tap Water Setup
  • Trauma & Burn Kits

Key Features

  • DOSH Compliance: All setups are designed to meet the Guidelines on First-Aid in the Workplace by the Department of Occupational Safety and Health (DOSH) Malaysia.
  • Durable Medical Furniture: Includes high-quality examination couches, medical cabinets, and waste management systems designed for industrial environments.
  • Comprehensive Emergency Kits: Fully stocked trauma bags, burn dressings, and spinal immobilization equipment for rapid response.
  • Professional Layout Design: We assist in planning the optimal room layout for accessibility, sanitation, and efficient patient treatment.
