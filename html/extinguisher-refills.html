Extinguisher Refills & Parts

Comprehensive refilling and maintenance services for all types of fire extinguishers.

Service & Product Overview

Airgas Technology provides certified fire extinguisher refilling and maintenance services. We ensure your fire protection equipment is always ready for use and compliant with BOMBA and SIRIM standards. We also supply a full range of high-quality replacement parts for various extinguisher types.

Service Capabilities

  • Refills: Dry Powder, CO2, Foam
  • Hydrostatic Testing
  • Internal Inspection
  • Valve Servicing
  • Weight Verification
  • BOMBA License Renewal

Key Features

  • Certified Compliance: All refilling and testing are performed according to MS 1539 standards for fire extinguishers.
  • High-Quality Media: We use premium suppressants (ABC powder, pure CO2, AFFF foam) to ensure maximum firefighting effectiveness.
  • Genuine Parts: Replacement of valves, gauges, hoses, and nozzles with high-quality, durable components.
  • Expert Maintenance: Experienced technicians provide thorough inspections and detailed service reports.
