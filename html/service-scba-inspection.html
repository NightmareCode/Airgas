SCBA & EEBD Inspection

Ensuring your life-saving equipment is ready when you need it most.

Comprehensive Breathing Apparatus Service

Airgas Technology provides certified inspection, maintenance, and repair services for Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) and Emergency Escape Breathing Devices (EEBD).

Our workshop is equipped to handle equipment from major manufacturers like MSA, Dräger, and Honeywell. We strictly adhere to manufacturer guidelines and international safety standards (DOSH, SIRIM) to ensure every unit performs flawlessly in hazardous environments.

Our Inspection Process Includes:

  • Visual Inspection: Checking straps, buckles, hoses, and backplates for wear, tears, or chemical damage.
  • Pneumatic Testing: Verifying pressure gauge accuracy, whistle warning activation, and reducer performance.
  • Face Mask Testing: Leak tightness test, exhalation valve check, and hygienic cleaning.
  • Cylinder Check: verifying hydrostatic test dates and valve integrity.
  • Flow Rate Analysis: Ensuring the air supply meets the required litres per minute (LPM) for safe breathing.

Why Regular Inspection Matters

SCBA and EEBD units are critical life-support devices. Rubber components degrade over time, and regulators can drift. Regular annual servicing ensures compliance with safety regulations and guarantees that the equipment will function correctly during an emergency.
