Airline System Service

Maintenance for breathing air trolleys and long-duration supply systems.

Continuous Air Supply You Can Trust

We service airline trolley systems and escape sets used for long-duration work in confined spaces or hazardous atmospheres where standard SCBA cylinders are insufficient.

Airline systems involve complex pneumatics, including long hoses, reels, pressure reducers, and automatic switchover valves. Regular maintenance is critical to prevent pressure drops or supply interruptions that could endanger workers.

Service Scope:

  • Trolley Inspection: Checking the frame, wheels, and cylinder mounting straps for stability and damage.
  • Hose Integrity: Pressure testing supply hoses for leaks, kinks, or chemical degradation.
  • Regulator Testing: Calibrating first and second-stage regulators to ensure consistent positive pressure to the face mask.
  • Escape Cylinder Check: verifying the readiness of the backup escape cylinder (typically 10-15 minutes duration).
  • Switchover Valve Test: Ensuring the system automatically switches to the backup cylinder if the primary supply fails.
