Apollo VTN 50LX2 Airline Trolley

Mobile breathing air trolley system for extended-duration work in hazardous zones.

Product Overview

The Apollo VTN 50LX2 breathing air trolley is designed to provide safe, portable air supply for workers operating in hazardous atmospheres. It supports two users simultaneously and is ideal for confined spaces, tank entry, and industrial maintenance work.

Typical Specifications

  • Two 50L high-pressure cylinders
  • Outlet for up to two users
  • Integrated pressure regulation
  • Sturdy trolley frame with wheels
  • Pressure gauge and warning markings
  • EN 14593 compliant components

Typical Applications

  • Tank and vessel entry: Long-duration maintenance in confined spaces.
  • Industrial cleaning: Sandblasting, chemical cleaning, and coating work.
  • Emergency response: Support system for hazmat and fire brigade operations.
