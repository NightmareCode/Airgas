ESAB Rogue ES 200i Inverter

Professional-level MMA and TIG welding in a compact, portable package.

⚡

Product Overview

The ESAB Rogue ES 200i is a robust and durable power source for the professional welder. The machine provides state-of-the-art welding performance and reliability through use of the latest high performance power electronics and digital control; ensuring a precise consistent arc. It's designed for use in the most demanding environments.

Technical Specifications

  • Current Range: 10 - 200 A (Stick/TIG)
  • Input: 230 V, 1ph, 50/60Hz
  • Weight: 8.4 kg (18.5 lbs)
  • Duty Cycle: 25% @ 200A
  • Max Electrode: 4.0 mm Ø
  • Protection: IP23S

Key Features

  • Superior Arc Characteristics: Smooth, stable performance with all types of MMA electrodes and Live TIG start.
  • Digital Display: Large, easy-to-read display ensures precise current settings even from a distance.
  • Portable Design: Lightweight and equipped with a shoulder strap for easy transport around the job site.
  • Robust Housing: Impact-resistant case and IP23S rating make it ideal for field work and outdoor applications.
