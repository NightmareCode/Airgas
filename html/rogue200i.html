ESAB Rogue ES 200i

High-performance MMA and TIG inverter machine designed for professional fabrication.

Product Overview

The ESAB Rogue ES 200i is a robust and durable power source for the professional welder. The machine provides state-of-the-art welding performance and reliability through the use of the latest high-performance power electronics and digital control, ensuring a precise and consistent arc.

Technical Specifications

  • Output Range: 10 – 200 Amps
  • Duty Cycle: 200A @ 25%
  • Input Voltage: 230V, 1ph
  • Weight: 8.4kg
  • Protection: IP23S
  • Electrodes: Up to 4.0mm Ø

Key Features

  • PFC (Power Factor Control): Ensures a stable arc and resistance to power fluctuations, even when working with long extension cables.
  • Adjustable Hot Start & Arc Force: Fine-tune the arc characteristics to suit the specific job and electrode type.
  • Digital Display: Large, easy-to-read display for precise amperage control.
  • Rugged Housing: Fiberglass-molded housing is built to withstand impact and harsh site conditions.
