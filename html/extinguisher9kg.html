9kg ABC Dry Powder

The standard multi-purpose fire extinguisher for residential and industrial fire protection.

Product Overview

The 9kg ABC Dry Powder Fire Extinguisher is the most versatile and effective solution for combating fires in diverse environments. Fully certified by SIRIM and BOMBA Malaysia, it is capable of tackling Class A (solid combustibles), Class B (flammable liquids), and Class C (flammable gases) fires, making it the ideal choice for warehouses, factories, and commercial premises.

Technical Specifications

  • Capacity: 9.0kg
  • Medium: ABC Dry Powder
  • Certification: SIRIM & BOMBA
  • Fire Rating: 27A / 144B
  • Operating Temp: -20°C to +60°C
  • Discharge Range: 4 – 6 meters

Key Features

  • SIRIM & BOMBA Certified: Fully compliant with Malaysian safety regulations, ensuring reliability and legal compliance for your premises.
  • Multi-Purpose Protection: Effective against most common types of fires, providing comprehensive first-response fire suppression.
  • Easy Monitoring: Equipped with a built-in pressure gauge for quick visual verification of the extinguisher's readiness.
  • Durable Construction: High-quality steel cylinder with corrosion-resistant coating for a long service life in industrial conditions.
