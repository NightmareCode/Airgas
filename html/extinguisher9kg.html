9kg ABC Dry Powder Extinguisher

SIRIM & BOMBA certified multi-purpose protection for homes and businesses.

Product Overview

Our 9kg ABC Dry Powder fire extinguisher is the most versatile and commonly used fire protection device in Malaysia. It is highly effective on Class A (solid combustibles), Class B (flammable liquids), and Class C (flammable gases) fires. Fully certified by SIRIM and approved by BOMBA, it is essential for warehouses, factories, and residential buildings.
