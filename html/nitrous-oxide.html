Anesthetic Gas

Nitrous Oxide (N2O)

High-purity medical gas for anesthesia and pain management.

N₂O

Product Overview

Nitrous Oxide, commonly known as laughing gas, is an effective analgesic and anesthetic agent. It is mixed with oxygen for induction and maintenance of anesthesia in surgical and dental procedures.

Specifications

  • Purity: ≥ 99.0% Medical Grade
  • Cylinder Color: Blue
  • Valve Type: Pin Index / Handwheel
  • Sizes: C, D, E, F, G Cylinders
  • State: Liquefied Gas

Applications

  • General Anesthesia: Used as a carrier gas in operating theaters.
  • Dentistry: Conscious sedation for anxiety relief.
  • Pain Management: Used in obstetrics (Entonox) and emergency care.
