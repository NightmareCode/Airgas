MSA V-Gard Helmets

The iconic industrial safety helmet trusted worldwide for consistent impact protection.

Product Overview

The MSA V-Gard® Helmet, with its distinctive trademark "V" design, is a world-recognized brand known for comfort, quality, and durability. MSA has sold well over 100 million V-Gard Helmets since its introduction – so many in fact, that the iconic "V" design has been a mainstay for many years on even the toughest worksites.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Polyethylene shell
  • Suspension: Fas-Trac III Ratchet
  • Weight: ~340g (Standard)
  • Standards: ANSI Z89.1, CSA Z94.1
  • Class: Type I, Class E (Non-vented)
  • Size: Standard (6 1/2" – 8")

Key Features

  • Iconic "V" Design: Provides top impact protection and structural rigidity while maintaining a low profile.
  • Fas-Trac® III Suspension: Features a smooth ratchet for easy adjustment, even with gloved hands, and a sweatband for comfort.
  • Accessory Integration: Includes standard slots for easy mounting of MSA ear muffs, face shields, and other accessories.
  • Customizable: Available in various colors and can be customized with company logos and reflective stripes.
