Airline Facemask with Escape Set

Continuous airline breathing protection with integrated escape cylinder for emergency exit.

Product Overview

This airline facemask system supplies breathing-quality air from a central source and includes a small escape cylinder to allow safe exit if the primary supply is interrupted. Ideal for confined spaces and extended-duration work in hazardous atmospheres.

Typical Specifications

  • Positive pressure full face mask
  • Compatible with airline and SCBA
  • Integrated 10–15 min escape cylinder
  • Anti-scratch panoramic visor
  • Antistatic hose options
  • EN 14593/EN 402 compliant

Typical Applications

  • Confined space entry: Tanks, pits, and process vessels with poor ventilation.
  • Chemical handling: Long-duration work around toxic gases or vapours.
  • Industrial maintenance: Sandblasting, painting, and cleaning in hazardous zones.
