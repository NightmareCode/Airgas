Aceweld MIG/TIG Inverters

Durable and efficient welding machines for diverse industrial applications.

Product Overview

Aceweld provides a comprehensive range of MIG, TIG, and Multi-Process inverter welding machines designed for both professional fabrication and industrial maintenance. Known for their stability and efficiency, Aceweld inverters deliver consistent results across various metal types including steel, stainless steel, and aluminum.

Technical Specifications

  • Output Current: 20A – 200A
  • Technology: IGBT Inverter
  • Input Voltage: 110V/220V Dual
  • Protection: IP21S / Overheat
  • Efficiency: ~85%
  • Cooling: Built-in Fan System

Key Features

  • Multi-Process Capability: Supports MIG, Flux-Cored, Lift TIG, and MMA (Stick) welding in a single compact unit.
  • IGBT Inverter Technology: Provides higher reliability and reduced energy loss while maintaining a stable welding arc.
  • Portable Design: Lightweight and compact housing with integrated handle for easy transport between job sites.
  • Synergic Control: Simplifies setup by automatically adjusting voltage and wire feed speed based on material thickness.
