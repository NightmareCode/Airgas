Fire Safety

2kg CO2 Extinguisher

Clean agent protection for electrical fires and sensitive equipment.

Product Overview

The 2kg CO2 (Carbon Dioxide) fire extinguisher is designed specifically for use on Class B (flammable liquid) and electrical fires. CO2 is a non-conductive, non-corrosive clean agent that leaves no residue after discharge, making it the perfect choice for server rooms, offices with computers, and laboratories.

Technical Specifications

  • Capacity: 2 kg
  • Fire Rating: 34B (Class B & Electrical)
  • Temperature: -30°C to +60°C
  • Discharge Time: 6 - 9 seconds
  • Cylinder: Seamless Steel / Aluminum
  • Agent: Carbon Dioxide (CO2)

Key Features

  • No Residue: The CO2 agent evaporates completely, eliminating the need for cleanup and preventing damage to sensitive electronics.
  • Non-Conductive: Safe for use on live electrical equipment, providing critical protection for server rooms and control panels.
  • Lightweight Aluminum: Our 2kg units use aluminum cylinders which are easier to handle and resistant to corrosion in humid environments.
  • Frost-Free Horn: Equipped with a specialized discharge horn designed to prevent frostbite during operation.
