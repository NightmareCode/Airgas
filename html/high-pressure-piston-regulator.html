High Pressure Piston Regulator

Heavy-duty regulator designed for reliable gas flow control in industrial settings.

Product Overview

This high pressure piston regulator is built to withstand demanding industrial environments while delivering stable outlet pressure from high-pressure gas cylinders or manifolds.

Typical Specifications

  • Single-stage piston design
  • Inlet pressure up to 300 bar
  • Adjustable outlet pressure range
  • Brass or stainless steel body
  • Inlet/Outlet gauges for easy monitoring
  • Compatible with common gas types

Typical Applications

  • Process gas supply: Regulating gases for production lines and test benches.
  • Gas distribution panels: Manifold regulation for multiple take-off points.
  • Laboratory and R&D: Stable supply for analytical instruments.
