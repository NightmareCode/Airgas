Height Safety

Working at Height Gear

Fall protection and rescue equipment for high-elevation industrial work.

Product Overview

Our comprehensive range of fall protection equipment is designed to ensure the safety of workers operating at height. From full-body harnesses to self-retracting lifelines, every product is engineered to meet rigorous international safety standards, providing reliability and peace of mind in challenging environments.

Technical Specifications

  • Harnesses: 2/3/5 Point Attachment
  • Lanyards: Shock Absorbing / Twin
  • Lifelines: 2m to 30m lengths
  • Material: High-Tenacity Polyester
  • Hardware: Forged Steel / Aluminum
  • Standards: EN 361, EN 355, EN 360

Key Features

  • Ergonomic Comfort: Padded harnesses and adjustable straps ensure worker comfort during long shifts at elevation.
  • High Durability: UV-resistant webbing and corrosion-resistant hardware for long-term use in harsh industrial settings.
  • Reliable Fall Arrest: Advanced shock-absorbing technology reduces impact forces on the body in the event of a fall.
  • Rescue Compatibility: Integrated attachment points for rescue systems and emergency evacuation.
