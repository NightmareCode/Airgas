Compressor Maintenance

Expert servicing for high-pressure breathing air compressors.

Pure Air Starts with a Healthy Compressor

Airgas Technology offers routine maintenance and major overhaul services for high-pressure breathing air compressors used in diving, firefighting, and industrial applications.

A neglected compressor can introduce contaminants like oil mist, carbon monoxide, or moisture into breathing air cylinders, posing serious health risks. Our technicians ensure your compressor runs efficiently and produces air that meets international purity standards (EN 12021).

Maintenance Checklist:

  • Oil Change: Using high-grade synthetic compressor oil to reduce friction and heat.
  • Filter Replacement: Changing intake filters and final stage purification cartridges (activated carbon/molecular sieve).
  • Belt & Pulley Check: Inspecting drive belts for wear and proper tension.
  • Valve Inspection: Cleaning and checking interstage and final stage valves for carbon buildup.
  • Air Quality Analysis: Testing output air for O2, CO2, CO, Oil, and Water content.
