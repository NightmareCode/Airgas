SCBA Cylinder Testing

Professional hydrostatic testing and inspection services to ensure the safety and compliance of your high-pressure cylinders.

Service Overview

High-pressure cylinders for SCBA, EEBD, and medical oxygen must undergo regular testing to ensure structural integrity. Airgas Technology provides comprehensive hydrostatic testing and visual inspection services in accordance with SIRIM and DOSH requirements.

Service Specifications

  • Test Method: Hydrostatic Water Jacket
  • Test Pressure: up to 450 bar
  • Frequency: Every 5 years (Standard)
  • Materials: Steel, Aluminum, Composite
  • Compliance: SIRIM, DOSH, ISO 18119
  • Lead Time: 3-5 working days

Key Service Features

  • Internal Visual Inspection: Thorough examination for corrosion, pitting, or contaminants using specialized fiber-optic equipment.
  • Hydrostatic Testing: Precise measurement of permanent and elastic expansion under pressure to confirm structural integrity.
  • Valve Maintenance: Ultrasonic cleaning, O-ring replacement, and function testing of the cylinder valve.
  • Certification & Tagging: Issuance of detailed test reports and durable tagging showing the next test date.
