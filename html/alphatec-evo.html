Hazmat

AlphaTec EVO Type CV

Encapsulating gas-tight suit offering top-tier protection against hazardous gases and liquids.

Product Overview

The AlphaTec EVO Type CV is a fully encapsulating chemical protective suit designed for the most demanding hazmat interventions. It combines excellent chemical resistance with a comfortable, ergonomic design to support long-duration missions.

Typical Specifications

  • Type 1a/1b gas-tight suit
  • SCBA worn inside the suit
  • Double zip with storm flap
  • Integral gloves and socks/boots
  • High-visibility visor
  • Approved to EN standards

Typical Applications

  • Chemical spill response: Protection during unknown or highly toxic leaks.
  • Industrial Hazmat: Reactor entry, transfer line maintenance, and decontamination.
  • Emergency response: Fire brigades and ERT teams handling hazardous substances.
