Hearing Protection

Cap Mount Earmuffs

High-performance noise-cancelling earmuffs compatible with safety helmets.

Product Overview

Cap mount earmuffs snap directly onto compatible safety helmets to provide reliable hearing protection without interfering with other PPE such as visors or face shields.

Typical Specifications

  • Helmet-mounted design
  • Soft, cushioned ear cups
  • Adjustable headband pressure
  • SNR/NRR noise reduction rating
  • Compatible with popular helmet brands
  • Meets EN352 or equivalent

Typical Applications

  • Industrial plants: Process areas, compressors, and pump rooms.
  • Construction: Piling, demolition, and heavy machinery operations.
  • Utilities: Power generation and maintenance activities.
