Industrial Gas Cylinders

High-pressure cylinders for all industrial gas needs, available in multiple sizes.

Product Overview

We supply a wide range of industrial gas cylinders designed to meet the rigorous safety and performance standards of the Malaysian industrial sector. Our cylinders are available for various gases including Oxygen, Nitrogen, Argon, CO2, Acetylene, and mixed gases, supporting everything from portable welding kits to large-scale industrial manufacturing.

Technical Specifications

  • Capacities: 10L, 40L, 47L, 50L
  • Working Pressure: 150 – 300 bar
  • Material: High-grade Seamless Steel
  • Standards: ISO 9809, BS 5045
  • Color Coding: SIRIM Standard
  • Valves: BS 341 Standard

Key Features

  • High-Pressure Safety: Each cylinder undergoes rigorous hydrostatic testing and inspection to ensure integrity at maximum working pressures.
  • Standardized Color Coding: Clearly identified shoulder and body colors in compliance with SIRIM standards for easy gas type identification.
  • Multiple Sizes: From lightweight 10L portable cylinders to high-capacity 50L industrial bundles to suit any requirement.
  • Quality Valves & Fittings: Equipped with premium valves that ensure a gas-tight seal and compatibility with standard industrial regulators.
