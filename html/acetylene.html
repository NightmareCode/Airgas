Fuel Gas

Dissolved Acetylene (C2H2)

The premium fuel gas for high-temperature welding, cutting, and heating.

Product Overview

Acetylene produces the highest flame temperature of all fuel gases (approx. 3,100°C) when mixed with oxygen. It provides a focused, high-intensity flame that allows for faster pre-heating and piercing times, making it the superior choice for oxy-fuel processes.

Technical Specifications

  • Purity: ≥ 98.0%
  • Storage: Dissolved in Acetone
  • Cylinder Color: Maroon
  • Valve Outlet: BS 341 No. 2
  • Flame Temp: ~3,100°C
  • Sizes: 5kg, 7kg, 10kg contents

Key Advantages

  • Fastest Pre-heating: Significantly reduces start-up time for cutting thick plates.
  • Versatile: Suitable for welding, cutting, brazing, soldering, and flame hardening.
  • High Efficiency: Consumes less oxygen per volume of fuel compared to propane.

Safety Warning

Acetylene is highly flammable. Store cylinders upright and use flashback arrestors at all times.
