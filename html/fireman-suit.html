Fireman Protection Suit

Professional heat-resistant apparel for structural firefighting and rescue operations.

Product Overview

Our Fireman Protection Suits are engineered to provide maximum thermal protection and comfort for firefighters in the most extreme conditions. Complying with the EN 469:2020 standard, these suits feature a multi-layer construction designed to protect against flame lick, radiant heat, and liquid chemical penetration while allowing for essential breathability.

Technical Specifications

  • Standard: EN 469:2020 Certified
  • Layers: 4-Layer Construction
  • Outer Shell: Nomex® / Aramid Blend
  • Moisture Barrier: PTFE Membrane
  • Thermal Barrier: Aramid Felt
  • Weight: Less than 3.5kg (Set)

Key Features

  • Advanced Multi-Layer System: Features an outer shell for flame resistance, a moisture barrier for waterproof protection, and a thermal lining for heat insulation.
  • Ergonomic Design: Two-piece garment (jacket and trousers) designed for maximum mobility during reaching, crawling, and climbing maneuvers.
  • High Visibility: Equipped with 3M™ Scotchlite™ reflective tapes for enhanced safety in low-visibility or smoky environments.
  • Reinforced Protection: Includes reinforced elbow and knee pads for added durability in high-wear areas during rescue operations.
