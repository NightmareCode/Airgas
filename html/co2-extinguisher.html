Fire Safety

CO2 Fire Extinguisher

Clean agent fire suppression for electrical equipment and flammable liquids.

Product Overview

Carbon Dioxide (CO2) extinguishers are the preferred choice for electrical fires (Class E) and flammable liquid fires (Class B). The gas is non-conductive, non-corrosive, and leaves no residue after discharge, protecting sensitive equipment.

Specifications

  • Capacity: 2kg, 5kg, 10kg, 45kg (Trolley)
  • Agent: Carbon Dioxide (CO2)
  • Cylinder: Seamless Steel / Aluminum
  • Certification: SIRIM / BOMBA
  • Range: 1 - 3 meters

Key Benefits

  • Clean Agent: Leaves no messy residue to clean up.
  • Electrical Safe: Does not conduct electricity; safe for live equipment.
  • Effective: Rapidly displaces oxygen to smother fires.

BOMBA Compliance

Ensure your premises meet Malaysia's Fire Services Act requirements with our certified extinguishers.
