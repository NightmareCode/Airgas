Industrial Argon

Ultra-high purity argon gas for precision welding and specialized industrial processes.

Ar

Product Overview

Argon is a colorless, odorless, non-reactive, inert gas. Airgas Technology provides high-purity argon that is essential for various industrial applications, particularly as a shielding gas in TIG and MIG welding to protect the weld area from atmospheric gases.

Technical Specifications

  • Purity: 99.999% (High Purity)
  • Moisture Content: < 2 ppm
  • Oxygen Content: < 2 ppm
  • Cylinder Size: 47L / 50L
  • Filling Pressure: 150 bar / 200 bar
  • Valve: BS No. 3 (Standard Bullnose)

Key Features

  • Inert Protection: Provides an excellent inert atmosphere for welding and metal processing, preventing oxidation.
  • High Purity: 99.999% purity ensures consistent results in sensitive electronics and analytical applications.
  • Stable Arc: Enhances arc stability and reduces spatter during welding operations.
  • Versatile Supply: Available in various cylinder sizes to suit different consumption levels.
