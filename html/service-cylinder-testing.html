Hydrostatic Cylinder Testing

Certified pressure testing for industrial and breathing air cylinders.

Ensure Your Cylinders are Safe

We provide professional hydrostatic testing services for a wide range of high-pressure gas cylinders, ensuring they meet the structural integrity requirements for safe refilling and use.

According to Malaysian regulations (DOSH), high-pressure gas cylinders must undergo periodic hydrostatic testing (typically every 5 years) to detect leaks, structural weaknesses, or metal fatigue that could lead to catastrophic failure.

Our Testing Capabilities:

  • Cylinder Types: SCBA (Carbon Composite), EEBD, Industrial Steel (Oxygen, Nitrogen, Argon, CO2), and Aluminium Medical cylinders.
  • Visual Inspection: Internal and external inspection for rust, pitting, dents, or bulges.
  • Pressure Test: Water jacket method to measure volumetric expansion under high pressure (1.5x working pressure).
  • Valve Service: Inspection and servicing of cylinder valves, with replacement if necessary.
  • Certification: Official test certificate and stamping of the test date on the cylinder shoulder upon passing.
