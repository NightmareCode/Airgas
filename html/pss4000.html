Dräger PSS 4000 SCBA

High-performance breathing apparatus for professional firefighting and industrial rescue.

Product Overview

The Dräger PSS 4000 is one of the lightest professional self-contained breathing apparatus for firefighters. Combining comfort with exceptional pneumatic performance, this SCBA is designed for applications where simplicity and ease of use are crucial. Lightweight yet robust, and easy to don, the PSS 4000 provides outstanding breathing protection.

Technical Specifications

  • Weight: 3.0 kg (Pneumatic) / 3.15 kg (Tx)
  • Input Pressure: 0 - 300 bar
  • Dimensions: 590 x 290 x 160 mm
  • Whistle Level: >90 dBa
  • Approvals: EN 137:2006 Type 2, ATEX
  • 1st Stage Flow: >1000 l/min

Key Features

  • Ergonomic Carrying System: Advanced harness design follows the natural contours of the body, reducing back strain and fatigue.
  • Integrated Hose Channels: Medium and high-pressure hoses are integrated into the carrying frame, preventing snagging during operations.
  • Robust Material: The harness and waist belt are made from high-performance materials that are resistant to heat, flames, and chemicals.
  • Easy Maintenance: The PSS 4000 is designed for quick and easy servicing, reducing downtime and maintenance costs.
