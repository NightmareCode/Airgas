AIRGAS TECHNOLOGY - PROJECT KNOWLEDGE BASE

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
------------------
The Airgas Technology Landing Page is a high-performance, responsive single-page application (SPA) serving as a digital product catalog for industrial safety equipment and gas solutions. It is designed for maximum speed and portability using a "Vanilla" web stack.

2. CORE TECH STACK & ARCHITECTURE
-------------------------------
- Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 (Modern Industrial Design), Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+).
- Data Layer: Centralized 'assets/products.json' store.
- Backend/Automation: Python 3.x scripts for data synchronization and asset processing.
- Design System: "Modern Industrial" aesthetic utilizing 'Space Grotesk' for headings and 'Instrument Sans' for body text. Features include glassmorphism, staggered animations, and reactive UI elements.

3. DATA PIPELINE & SYNCHRONIZATION
--------------------------------
- Primary Source: Data is fetched directly from the official Airgas website (https://airgas.my/) via the WordPress REST API (wp-json/wp/v2/product).
- Synchronization (tools/fetch_real_products.py): 
    * Automates deep fetching of product titles, links, descriptions, and featured images.
    * Implements keyword-based logic to map products to specific Industries and Sub-Categories.
- Knowledge Enrichment (tools/update_knowledge.py):
    * Performs sophisticated brand mapping using a comprehensive keyword library (MSA, Honeywell, Ansell, etc.).
    * Injects "Industry Descriptions" and "Sub-Category Knowledge" points directly into the JSON data.

4. ASSET MANAGEMENT & CONVENTIONS
-------------------------------
- Folder Structure: Images are organized into industry-specific folders (e.g., 'assets/ConfinedSpace/', 'assets/Welding/').
- Naming Convention: Product names are sanitized (alphanumeric only, trimmed) and mapped to .png files.
- Fallback System: The UI implements a three-tier fallback for images:
    1. Local asset (industry-specific folder).
    2. Remote URL (fetched from WordPress).
    3. UI-Avatars (initials-based placeholder).
- Maintenance Tools:
    * image_trimmer.py: Removes empty whitespace from product images.
    * image_enhancer.py: Improves image clarity/quality.
    * sync_assets.py: Ensures the local folder structure matches the JSON data.

5. UI/UX FEATURES
----------------
- Industry Tabs: Large, pill-shaped filters for quick switching between categories.
- Sub-Category Grouping: Products are logically grouped within their industries.
- Knowledge Boxes: Technical "Did You Know?" sections for each sub-category to educate users on safety standards and equipment utility.
- Interaction: 3D-like hover effects on product cards and a modern search engine with real-time results.

6. CORE PRODUCT CATEGORIES
------------------------
- Confined Space: SCBA, detectors, rescue systems, and escape sets.
- Oil & Gas: Chemical suits, FR gear, and energy-sector tools.
- Welding: Machines (MIG/TIG/ARC), electrodes, and accessories.
- Gases & Equipment: Industrial/medical gases, cylinders, regulators, and manifolds.
- Gas Calibration: Calibration gases and specialized sensors.
- PPE: Workplace protection (helmets, gloves, eyewear, vests).
- Services & Maintenance: Hydro testing, calibration, and certification.
- Maritime & Offshore: Marine survival gear and offshore-certified apparatus.

7. COMPANY SPECIALIZATION (Airgas Technology Sdn Bhd)
---------------------------------------------------
- Role: One-stop supply center for safety and gas solutions in Malaysia.
- Locations: Showroom in Pasir Gudang (Johor) and a specialized workshop in Nilai (Negeri Sembilan).
- Key Capabilities: Refilling (Medical O2/Air, CO2), Hydro-testing (up to 450bar), Posicheck testing, and technical safety training.
- Value: Authorized distributor for premium global brands, ensuring products meet ANSI, CE, or EN standards.
