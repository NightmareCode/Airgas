Airgas Technology Sdn Bhd is a "one-stop supply" center for industrial and medical gases, high-quality safety equipment (PPE), and certified technical training in Malaysia.

We have a main showroom in Pasir Gudang (Johor) and a specialized technical workshop in Nilai (Negeri Sembilan).
