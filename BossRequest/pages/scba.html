Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA)

Providing autonomous breathable air in IDLH atmospheres. Built for extreme industrial, petrochemical, and firefighting conditions.

System Components

Explore the core elements of our positive pressure SCBA systems, engineered for maximum safety and ergonomic comfort.

Zenith Lung Demand Valve

  • Positive pressure with automatic activation upon facepiece connection.
  • By-pass button mode offering 450 l/min flow.
  • Dual-axis rotation for easy hose positioning.
  • Quick release via dual buttons.

Pano Face Mask

  • Lightweight design (560g) for extended wear.
  • Skirt and inner mask constructed from durable EPDM.
  • Universal fit engineered for ASEAN face profiles.
  • Wide 360-degree field of vision.

Backplate & Harnesses

  • Ergonomically designed for professional firefighting.
  • Flame retardant Polyaramid-Fibre Glass construction.
  • EN137:2006 Type 2 compliant (self-extinguishing).
  • Nomex/Kevlar cushioning with high visibility straps.

Composite Cylinders

  • Lightweight carbon composite with aluminium liner.
  • EN12245 approved with a 15-year shelf life.
  • Built-in pressure gauge on the cylinder valve.
  • Right hand wheel design protected by rubber caps.

Mechanical Gauge & Alarm

  • EN837-1 certified (Class 1.6 precision).
  • Integrated whistle alarm in the handle (90 dB output).
  • Triggers at 55 ± 5 Bar remaining.
  • Dual units measurement (Bar & Mpa).

SCBA Maintenance & Testing

Proper maintenance is critical for life-saving equipment. We offer comprehensive workshop services for SCBA gear.

Testing Capabilities

  • Visual inspections and posicheck verification.
  • Cylinder hydraulic volumetric expansion testing.
  • High and medium pressure leakage tests.
  • Response pressure warning device calibration.
  • Mask & Demand Valve leakage tests in positive/negative pressure.
  • Cleaning, drying, relabeling, and gas refilling.
